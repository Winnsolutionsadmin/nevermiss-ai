FAQ

Questions We Get Every Day

Will my callers know they're talking to AI?
Your AI receptionist answers with your business name and sounds natural and conversational. We train it on your specific services, your language, your FAQs. Most callers don't realize — and frankly don't care, because they got their question answered instantly. We recommend full transparency if asked directly, and your AI is trained to acknowledge it when asked.
What if I have complex pricing or unusual services?
Your AI is trained on your exact business. We go deep in the onboarding session — pricing ranges, service scope, what you will and won't do, geographic limits, common objections. For anything outside its training, it collects the lead and flags it for human follow-up rather than guessing. You never lose the lead.
How long does setup actually take?
One 30-minute onboarding call with our team. We handle the technical setup — no IT required. You forward your existing number to us (2-minute process on your carrier's website). You're live within 24 hours. Most clients are fully operational same-day.
What integrations do you support?
We integrate with 50+ tools including: Google Calendar, Calendly, Acuity, HubSpot, Salesforce, JobNimbus, ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Pipedrive, and most major CRMs. If you use something we don't list, ask us — we build new integrations regularly. Webhook support is available on all plans.
What's the cancellation policy?
Month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No contracts, no cancellation fees. We're confident you'll see ROI in the first 30 days — but you're not locked in. The setup fee is non-refundable after your AI goes live (that's the labor to train and configure it).
